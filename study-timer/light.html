Why Use a Web Timer?

Unlike mobile apps, this web-based timer keeps you focused. No need to unlock your phone, no distracting notifications, no social media temptation. Keep your study tools in one browser tab while you write essays, research papers, or use the word counter.

How It Works

  • Focus during study sessions, relax during breaks
  • Track your daily progress and build consistency
  • Works offline - no login or downloads required
